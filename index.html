Experiential Psychodynamic Psychotherapy

A serene, supportive space for profound emotional exploration, authentic insight, and lasting personal transformation.

About This Practice

This private practice offers a compassionate, depth-oriented approach to psychotherapy. Inspired by experiential psychodynamic principles, the work centers on exploring your unique emotional world and relational patterns in a safe, non-judgmental environment.

The goal is to foster genuine self-understanding, emotional vitality, and more fulfilling connections.

What to Expect

In sessions, you are invited to:

  • Engage with feelings and experiences as they unfold in the moment
  • Illuminate patterns in relationships, past and present
  • Discover how early life shapes current emotional realities
  • Build deeper awareness and new relational possibilities
  • Experience organic, enduring shifts in well-being

Transformation arises through insight, lived experience, and the therapeutic relationship.

The Approach

Rooted in contemporary psychodynamic therapy—including Jonathan Shedler's evidence-based framework—this practice views the therapeutic relationship as a key catalyst for understanding and change.

Sessions are 50 minutes, usually weekly or as beneficial.
